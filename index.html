Oxyriva Holiday Resort, Kokan



Kokan, often known as Maharashtra's Kashmir, is a enchanting place situated on the western coast of India.

Beaches are the main attractions of Kokan due to their charm & beauty. When it comes to dream vacations, most of us picture ourselves on the golden sands of a beach, layed on a chair just inches from blue sparkling waves and refreshing drinks with little umbrellas brought to us as we Relax.

Oxyriva Holiday Resort is the Perfectly closest resort to the Scenic Diveagar Beach. We make your dream Vacation a reality by offering you sumptuous stays both AC - Non AC, complete with huge parking that help you unwind beside the majesty of the ocean.
